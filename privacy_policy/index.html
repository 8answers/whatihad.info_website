Privacy Policy

At whatihad.info, we value user privacy and aim to protect personal information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Activity level
  • Goals
  • Food preferences
  • Budget preferences
  • Other nutrition-related inputs

Users may also sign in using third-party authentication providers such as Google Sign-In. When using Google authentication, certain account information such as name, email address, and profile information may be shared with whatihad.info in accordance with the user’s Google account privacy settings.

Use of Google authentication is also subject to Google Privacy Policy and Google Terms of Service.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect device information, browser type, IP address, usage analytics, interaction data, and technical diagnostics.

whatihad.info may request optional access to device location information to improve Bellyo AI recommendations, food suggestions, regional pricing estimates, nearby food availability, hydration guidance, and overall user experience.

Location access is completely optional and users may choose to allow, deny, or revoke location permissions at any time through their device or browser settings.

If location access is disabled, certain personalized features or recommendations may have reduced accuracy or functionality.

We use collected data to:

  • Improve Bellyo AI
  • Personalize user experience
  • Improve food suggestions

  • Optimize platform functionality

User inputs may be processed through AI and machine learning systems to generate responses and improve recommendation quality.
