About whatihad.info

Healthy eating should not feel expensive, complicated, or unrealistic.

Most nutrition and calorie tracking apps focus heavily on calories, macros, and detailed analytics, but often ignore one important real-world factor:

Affordability.

For many people, food decisions are not only about nutrition goals they are also about budget, local food availability, country-specific pricing, and practicality. We are building Bellyo AI to help bridge that gap.

Why Bellyo AI is getting build

Many nutrition apps are designed around ideal meal plans that may not match real-world lifestyles or budgets.

People often struggle with questions like:

  • What can I eat within my budget?
  • What foods are available in my country?
  • How do I get enough protein affordably?
  • How can I eat healthier without overspending?

Bellyo AI is getting build to make nutrition guidance more practical, accessible, and realistic for everyday life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthier food choices simpler and more affordable for everyone.

We believe nutrition tools should:

  • Be easy to understand
  • Work across different countries and currencies
  • Support realistic food budgets
  • Help users make smarter everyday decisions
  • Avoid overwhelming complexity

Bellyo focuses on practical nutrition tracking combined with AI-powered food suggestions personalized to each user’s goals, budget, and country.
